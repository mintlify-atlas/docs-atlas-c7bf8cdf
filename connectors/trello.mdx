Connect Trello to Airweave to make your kanban boards searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The Trello connector syncs:

- **Boards** - Board metadata and settings
- **Lists** - Columns on boards
- **Cards** - Cards with full details
- **Checklists** - Card checklist items
- **Members** - Board members and collaborators

## Authentication

This connector uses **OAuth 1.0** authentication (Trello's authentication method).

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Trello Connection">
    In Airweave, navigate to your collection and click **Add Source** → **Trello**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Click **Connect** and authorize Airweave to access your Trello boards.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Airweave will begin syncing your Trello data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Entities

### Trello Cards

Cards with full context and metadata.

**Key fields:**
- Card name and description
- Board and list assignment
- Due dates and completion status
- Assigned members
- Labels and checklists
- Attachment count

### Trello Boards

Board-level organization.

**Key fields:**
- Board name and description
- Visibility settings
- Organization association
