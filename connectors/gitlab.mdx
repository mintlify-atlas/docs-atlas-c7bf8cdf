Connect GitLab repositories to Airweave to make your code searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The GitLab connector syncs repository data including files, commits, and metadata.

## Authentication

This connector uses **OAuth 2.0** authentication.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add GitLab Connection">
    In Airweave, navigate to your collection and click **Add Source** → **GitLab**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Click **Connect** and authorize Airweave to access your GitLab repositories.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Airweave will begin syncing your GitLab data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
