Connect ClickUp to Airweave to make your task management data searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The ClickUp connector syncs:

- **Workspaces** - Top-level organization containers
- **Spaces** - Workspace subdivisions
- **Folders** - Space folders
- **Lists** - Task lists
- **Tasks** - Tasks with full details
- **Subtasks** - Nested tasks
- **Comments** - Task discussions
- **Attachments** - Files attached to tasks

## Authentication

This connector uses **OAuth 2.0** authentication.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add ClickUp Connection">
    In Airweave, navigate to your collection and click **Add Source** → **ClickUp**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Click **Connect** and authorize Airweave to access your ClickUp workspaces.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Airweave will begin syncing your ClickUp data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Entities

### ClickUp Tasks

Complete task data including custom fields.

**Key fields:**
- Task name and description
- Status and priority
- Assignees and tags
- Due dates and time tracking
- Custom field values

### ClickUp Lists

Task list containers.

**Key fields:**
- List name and content
- Folder and space association
- Status configuration
- Task count
