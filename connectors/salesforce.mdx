Connect Salesforce to Airweave to make your CRM data searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The Salesforce connector syncs:

- **Accounts** - Companies and organizations
- **Contacts** - People and leads
- **Opportunities** - Deals and sales prospects

## Authentication

This connector uses **OAuth 2.0** with custom credentials.

## Configuration

### Instance URL (Auto-populated)

Your Salesforce instance domain is automatically extracted from the OAuth response.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Salesforce Connected App">
    In Salesforce Setup, create a Connected App with OAuth enabled.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Salesforce Connection">
    In Airweave, enter your Client ID and Client Secret.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Complete the OAuth flow to grant Airweave access to your Salesforce org.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Entities

### Salesforce Accounts

Company and organization records.

**Key fields:**
- Account name and number
- Industry and revenue
- Billing/shipping addresses
- Created/updated timestamps

### Salesforce Contacts

People and leads.

**Key fields:**
- Contact name and email
- Phone numbers
- Job title and department
- Associated account

### Salesforce Opportunities

Deals and sales pipeline.

**Key fields:**
- Opportunity name and amount
- Stage and probability
- Close date
- Associated account
