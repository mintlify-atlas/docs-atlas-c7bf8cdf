Connect Freshdesk to Airweave to make your customer support data searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The Freshdesk connector syncs support ticket data including tickets, conversations, and metadata.

## Authentication

Authentication method varies by Freshdesk configuration.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Freshdesk Connection">
    In Airweave, navigate to your collection and click **Add Source** → **Freshdesk**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Connection">
    Follow the prompts to set up authentication.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Airweave will begin syncing your Freshdesk data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
