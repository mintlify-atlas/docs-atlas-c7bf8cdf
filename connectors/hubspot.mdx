Connect HubSpot to Airweave to make your CRM and marketing data searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The HubSpot connector syncs:

- **Contacts** - Contact records with all properties
- **Companies** - Company records
- **Deals** - Deal pipeline data
- **Tickets** - Support tickets

## Authentication

This connector uses **OAuth 2.0** authentication.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add HubSpot Connection">
    In Airweave, navigate to your collection and click **Add Source** → **HubSpot**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Click **Connect** and authorize Airweave to access your HubSpot account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Airweave will begin syncing your HubSpot data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Entities

### HubSpot Contacts

Contact records with flexible properties.

**Key fields:**
- Name and email
- All custom properties
- Created/updated timestamps

### HubSpot Companies

Company records.

**Key fields:**
- Company name
- Domain
- All custom properties

### HubSpot Deals

Deal pipeline data.

**Key fields:**
- Deal name
- Amount
- All custom properties
