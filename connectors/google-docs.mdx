Connect Google Docs to Airweave to make your documents searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The Google Docs connector syncs document content from Google Docs files in your Google Drive.

<Note>
This connector works in conjunction with the Google Drive connector. Make sure you have Google Drive connected as well.
</Note>

## Authentication

This connector uses **OAuth 2.0** with custom credentials (same as Google Drive).

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Up Google Drive First">
    Follow the [Google Drive setup instructions](/connectors/google-drive) to configure OAuth.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Google Docs Connection">
    In Airweave, click **Add Source** → **Google Docs** and use the same OAuth credentials.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Complete the OAuth flow to grant access.
  </Step>
</Steps>
