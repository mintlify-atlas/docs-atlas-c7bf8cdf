Connect Pipedrive to Airweave to make your sales pipeline searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The Pipedrive connector syncs:

- **Persons** - Contacts
- **Organizations** - Companies
- **Deals** - Sales opportunities
- **Activities** - Tasks, calls, meetings
- **Products** - Product catalog
- **Leads** - Lead management
- **Notes** - Notes and comments

## Authentication

This connector uses **API Token** authentication.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Pipedrive API Token">
    In Pipedrive, go to Settings → Personal preferences → API to find your API token.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Pipedrive Connection">
    In Airweave, click **Add Source** → **Pipedrive** and enter your API token.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Airweave will begin syncing your Pipedrive data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Entities

### Pipedrive Deals

Sales opportunities with pipeline stage.

**Key fields:**
- Deal title and value
- Stage and pipeline
- Expected close date
- Associated person/organization

### Pipedrive Persons

Contact records.

**Key fields:**
- Name and email
- Phone number
- Associated organization
- All custom properties
