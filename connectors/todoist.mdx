Connect Todoist to Airweave to make your personal task management data searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The Todoist connector syncs:

- **Projects** - Project containers and metadata
- **Sections** - Project sections
- **Tasks** - Tasks with full details
- **Comments** - Task comments

## Authentication

This connector uses **OAuth 2.0** authentication.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Todoist Connection">
    In Airweave, navigate to your collection and click **Add Source** → **Todoist**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Click **Connect** and authorize Airweave to access your Todoist workspace.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Airweave will begin syncing your Todoist data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Entities

### Todoist Tasks

Tasks with full context and metadata.

**Key fields:**
- Task content and description
- Priority (1-4)
- Due date and recurrence
- Labels and project
- Assignee information

### Todoist Projects

Project organization containers.

**Key fields:**
- Project name and color
- View style (list or board)
- Sharing status
- Parent project (if nested)
