Connect Outlook Calendar to Airweave to make your Microsoft calendar events searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The Outlook Calendar connector syncs:

- **Calendars** - Calendar metadata
- **Events** - Calendar events with attendees
- **Attachments** - Event attachments

## Authentication

This connector uses **OAuth 2.0** via Microsoft Graph API.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Outlook Calendar Connection">
    In Airweave, navigate to your collection and click **Add Source** → **Outlook Calendar**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Click **Connect** and sign in with your Microsoft account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Airweave will begin syncing your calendar events.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Entities

### Outlook Calendar Events

Events with full meeting details.

**Key fields:**
- Subject and body
- Start/end date and time
- Attendees and organizer
- Location and online meeting URL
- Recurrence pattern
