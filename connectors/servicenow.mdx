Connect ServiceNow to Airweave to make your IT service management data searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The ServiceNow connector syncs ITSM data including incidents, requests, and configuration items.

## Authentication

Authentication method varies by ServiceNow configuration.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add ServiceNow Connection">
    In Airweave, navigate to your collection and click **Add Source** → **ServiceNow**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Connection">
    Follow the prompts to set up authentication.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Airweave will begin syncing your ServiceNow data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
