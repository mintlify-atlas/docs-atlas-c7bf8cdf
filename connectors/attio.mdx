Connect Attio to Airweave to make your modern CRM data searchable by AI agents.

## What Gets Synced

The Attio connector syncs:

- **Objects** - Custom CRM objects (Companies, People, Deals, etc.)
- **Lists** - Organized collections of records
- **Records** - Individual CRM records with attributes
- **Notes** - Notes attached to records

## Authentication

This connector uses **API Key** authentication.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Attio API Key">
    In Attio, go to Workspace Settings → Developers to generate an API key.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Attio Connection">
    In Airweave, click **Add Source** → **Attio** and enter your API key.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Sync">
    Airweave will begin syncing your Attio workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Entities

### Attio Records

Flexible CRM records with custom attributes.

**Key fields:**
- Record name and description
- Email addresses and phone numbers
- Custom attributes
- Associated object/list

### Attio Notes

Notes attached to records.

**Key fields:**
- Note title and content
- Author and timestamps
- Associated record
